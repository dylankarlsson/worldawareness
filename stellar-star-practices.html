Spiritual Protections for the Cancerian Supernova

By Sara Mitchell

March 15, 2024

This April we will observe the new birth of the Altarf Star, the Cancerian Supernova which will light up our skies in the Northern Hemisphere. Beginning on April 4th, 2026, following the spring equinox, this star will reveal its cosmic rebirth now visible on Earth, emanating its new, powerful and luminous form as one of the brightest stars in the sky. 303 light-years away, this Cancerian star is experiencing a stellar ascension into a higher state of being.

On April 4th, we are invited to witness and experience the opening of a portal within the cardinal sign of Cancer. Cancer is known for embodying deep emotional sensitivity, intense intuition, and a protective, nurturing nature. Ruled by the moon, Cancer represents home, family, comfort, and the ability to care for others, while often needing a hard "shell" for self-protection. This period of transformation is a time to protect and nurture the spiritual hearth.

A Stellar Transformation

A supernova transformation symbolizes the release of the highest frequency of light, inner soul awakening, and the realization of being part of universal consciousness, often marking a "second self-realization" of divine spirit within. This explosive transformation is a death and a rebirth, signaling an outpouring of cosmic energy and abundant potential.

Across all Cancer placements, we should notice an energetic shift towards this awakening. This sudden surge of illumination may feel like the transformation known well to Cancers: the softness of the crab after molting its protective shell. Sensitive to light and energetic interaction, the Cancerian Supernova is a time to reflect and strengthen the self in preparation for a transformed spiritual self.

Energetic Protections

For the night of April 4th, when the Altarf Star will reach its peak luminance, here are several practices to energetically protect and fortify the spiritual self:

1. Take time to reflect and engage in grounding practices: yoga, journaling and meditation offer energetic fortitude during this intense energetic transformation period.

2. With your body grounded and filled with positive energy, visualize a beam of light emerging from the top of your head connecting you to the Universal Being. The key is resonating with the frequencies provided by the Cancerian Supernova. Attract the right frequency to align yourself with this sacred and cosmic energy shift.

3. Place a mixture of salt, cloves, and cleansing herbs into a small cloth pouch and keep it under your pillow while sleeping to prevent a build up of negative energy.

4. Perform the Celtic Circle protective spell 3 times when the sun meets the horizon. Trace an invisible circle clockwise around yourself using your right index finger, starting from the ground up, to form an immediate protective barrier.

5. As a final form of protection, quarter a lemon and soak it in salt. Leave each quarter in the four corners of the dwelling space. This wards off and absorbs stagnant and malicious energies that may enter the home.

May you find the sacred in the spaces between your thoughts, and may silence become your most trusted teacher.

May your heart feel lighter after every visit.
